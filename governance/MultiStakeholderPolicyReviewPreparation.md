## 10. Multi-Stakeholder Policy Review Preparation

**Task:** Prepare policy draft for review by diverse stakeholders with different concerns and technical backgrounds

**Advanced Techniques:** Audience segmentation, concern anticipation, review guide creation, feedback integration planning

**Principles Applied:** Context (multiple audiences), Structure (review process), Specificity (stakeholder needs)

```markdown

- Policy Type: [NEW / MAJOR_REVISION / MINOR_UPDATE]
- Primary Driver: [COMPLIANCE_REQUIREMENT / SECURITY_INCIDENT / BUSINESS_NEED / PERIODIC_REVIEW]

---

STAKEHOLDER PREPARATION TASK:

Your goal is to prepare materials that enable efficient, informed review by diverse stakeholders:

**STEP 1: ANTICIPATE CONCERNS BY STAKEHOLDER GROUP**

For each stakeholder group listed above, think through:
- What specific provisions in this policy will concern them?
- What questions will they ask?
- What information do they need to make informed decisions?
- What objections might they raise?
- What data/evidence would address their concerns?

**STEP 2: CREATE AUDIENCE-APPROPRIATE CONTEXT**

Different audiences need different levels of detail:
- Executives: Business case, risk/benefit, resource requirements
- Legal: Regulatory mapping, compliance gaps addressed, liability considerations
- Technical: Implementation approach, tools needed, operational impact
- Business units: User experience, training needs, support available

**STEP 3: PREPARE SUPPORTING MATERIALS**

What materials will help each audience review effectively?
- Executive summary for leadership
- Regulatory mapping for legal
- Implementation guide for technical teams
- FAQ for business units
- Redline comparison (if policy revision)
```
